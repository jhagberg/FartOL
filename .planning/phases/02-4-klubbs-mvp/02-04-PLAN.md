| T-02P4-02 | Tampering       | malicious LAN injects competitors via auto-merge with crafted names | mitigate    | class-match guard limits to known classes (Test 8); names rendered via Svelte text interpolation (auto-escaped); operator confirms via consent-toast on first card_read (Phase 1 C-M4 path)                        |
